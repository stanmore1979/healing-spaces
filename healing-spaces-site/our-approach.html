Our Approach

Transforming vacant properties into centres of wellbeing, creativity, and social impact.

Mission

Healing Spaces repurposes underused and empty buildings into regenerative environments that support collective wellbeing. We create temporary homes and work studios for:

  • Wellness practitioners
  • Artists and architects
  • People excluded from conventional housing models

Our model is a new kind of property guardianship--one grounded in healing-centred design, community-building, and low-impact, socially beneficial use.

Wellness Architecture: A Philosophy of Care

We believe buildings should do more than house people--they should heal.

Core Design Principles:

  • Natural light and biophilic design
  • Quiet zones and acoustic comfort
  • Non-toxic materials and air quality
  • Flexible layouts for living and working
  • Community participation in design

Key Collaborators:

  • Trauma-informed architects
  • Regenerative designers
  • Social impact developers
  • Wellness educators

How It Works

For Landlords

  1. Property inspection and eligibility assessment
  2. Agreements signed with flexible terms
  3. Guardians move in under our supervision
  4. Property monitored, maintained, and improved
  5. Handover on agreed notice period

Benefits to You:

  • Secures from squatters, vandalism, and decay
  • Reduces insurance liabilities and costs
  • Flexible terms and quick termination
  • Enhances CSR/ESG reputation
  • Potential tax relief (jurisdiction-dependent)

For Guardians

Our guardians are wellness professionals, artists, and community builders often excluded from the housing market.

They receive an affordable, safe place to live and work--and bring energy and care to otherwise empty buildings.

FAQs for Landlords

Can I regain possession at any time?
Yes. Our agreements allow for flexible termination, usually with 28 days' notice.
Who pays utilities and upkeep?
Guardians cover utilities and daily use. We manage liaison, oversight, and basic repairs.
What kind of legal agreement is used?
Non-tenancy licence agreements that preserve your full rights as owner.
What if I plan to sell or develop the building?
We can vacate quickly, leaving the space improved and well-managed.
