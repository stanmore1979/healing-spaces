Welcome to Healing Spaces

We transform vacant and underused buildings into nurturing places to live and work, with a model grounded in care, stability, and long-term stewardship. Healing Spaces connects building owners with responsible guardians who protect, inhabit, and activate space in ways that serve wellbeing and community.

Our Approach Become a Guardian For Owners

Partner With Healing Spaces

Do you have an empty or underused property? We offer a trusted pathway to bring it back to life--caring for the building, ensuring it’s safely occupied, and aligning it with socially valuable use.

Guardianship through Healing Spaces offers peace of mind, careful oversight, and a sense of purpose. We work with owners who want to explore meaningful stewardship without the burdens of traditional letting or complex tenancy arrangements.

Our Process

We begin with a conversation--about your space, your concerns, and your vision. From there, we propose a bespoke guardianship plan that fits your building, timeline, and values.

  1. Initial Consultation: Understand your goals, risks, and requirements.
  2. Onboarding & Preparation: We inspect the building, discuss legal agreements, and prepare it for guardianship.
  3. Placement & Care: Carefully selected individuals move in. We provide ongoing support, inspections, and updates.
  4. Ongoing Partnership: We stay responsive, transparent, and aligned with your priorities.
